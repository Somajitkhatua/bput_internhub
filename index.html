About BPUT InternHub

BPUT InternHub is a dedicated platform connecting BPUT students with quality internship opportunities. Our AI-powered system ensures the perfect match between students and companies.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academia and industry by providing students with real-world experience and companies with talented interns.

Our Vision

To become the leading internship platform for technical universities in India.

Quality, Innovation, and Student Success are at the core of everything we do.
